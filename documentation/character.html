Click to start the quickstart tutorial

To create an inkling/octoling press Shift + A. Go to Mesh and click on Splatoon Player.

Your character should appear, expend the bottom left menue to access to customization parameters.

Before starting to customize your character I recommand to quickly setup a viewport to preview the character as it will appear in renders.

Press ; to move your view to the character. Rotate your view by holding the scroll to face the character. Select the viewport shading up right.

Finally go to the viewport shading options, disable "Scene World" and choose an HDRI (by clicking on the sphere).

Now you can visualize your character.

Explore all the possibilities and create the perfect character!

If you accidently close the customization menue without finishing just delete entirely the character and create it again: all your customization settings will reappear. That also allows you to create squid/octopus form for your character quickly.

Let's explore the armature to make poses and animations. This armature is game-accurate and easy to use.

You will notice there is 5 bones on the side, those bones are for shading effect like ink, squid/octopus transformation or ink level.
The first one enable smoothly the human-to-squid form.

The second bone define the amount of ally ink on the character.

The third one define the amount of ennemy ink on the character.

The fourth bone make the character human form transparent.

This bone is made to be used with the human-to-squid transformation to hide cloths and body smoothly.

The fifth bone define the ink tank level.

Another special bone is the hair sizer, next to the head. It's usefull for the human-to-squid transformation.
